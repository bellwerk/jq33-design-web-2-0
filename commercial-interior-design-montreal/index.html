FAQ

Quick answers before you book a call.

How do we get started?

Book the free 15-minute call. We confirm scope, timeline, and the best package for your space.

What do you need to prepare a quote?

Space type, approximate size, timeline, budget range, and any plans or photos you can share.

Do you work with my contractor?

Yes. Depending on the package, we provide layouts, finish direction, and visuals that can be shared for pricing and execution discussions.

Do you offer site visits in Montreal?

Yes. Site visits and measurements are available for Montreal projects depending on scope and package.

Do you provide 3D renders?

Yes. Signature Interior includes 2 key views, and the Contractor-Ready Package includes 3-4 views plus elevations.

How long does a commercial interior design project take?

Timelines depend on package: 7-10 business days for Layout Sprint, 3-4 weeks for Signature Interior, and 4-6 weeks for Contractor-Ready.

Can you work from an existing floor plan?

Yes. We can work from existing plans and verify details with photos or a site measurement if needed.

Is the first call free?

Yes. The 15-minute intro call is free and used to confirm fit and next steps.

Do you work only in Montreal?

Yes. We focus on Montreal and Greater Montreal for faster site support.

What kinds of businesses do you design for?

Cafes, salons, boutiques, clinics, studios, offices, and other small-to-mid commercial spaces.
