Brief / Concept
The goal was simple: make the space feel premium, photograph well, and work better day-to-day. We focused on a layout that guides customers naturally, creates one or two signature moments, and keeps the build realistic for budget and timeline.
Execution
We refined lighting intent, finishes, and key viewpoints so the space looks great from the entrance, at the counter, and from main seating and consult zones. Durable materials and clear handoff notes kept quoting straightforward and protected the design during value engineering.
